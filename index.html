Deco27 Quality Promise

The Highest Standards of Quality.

Quality has always been the most important part of our philosophy, and we take it very seriously. With a regularly trained staff, we ensure we are always up to date on the newest technology, materials, and installation processes.

Deco27 prides itself in offering unparalleled customer satisfaction by delivering solutions to the most creative interior designs and challenging installation alternatives. We are conveniently located in the heart of Miami, rendering our business an ideal destination for designers, architects and clients alike with high quality personalized attention to detail that have made us the leader in our field for more than 30 yrs.

We offer the most elegant and luxurious quality elements, like hand crafted hardwood floors and walls, recycled leather floors, porcelains, classic and extravagant doors, and even art. Our product line is characterized by their striking colors, diversity of rich styles, and unsurpassed durability.
